How to add your story?



Create an issue on Github with the following information attached:

  • your name and position
  • a link to your company website
  • your story

You can use the following list of questions to structure your story:

  • For what purposes do you use Alfresco?
  • How many people use Alfresco?
  • Why did you choose Alfresco? What Alfresco's key features were and are important for you?
  • Any problems you have encountered? Did you solve them all, or do you still have any difficulties? Any advice to us what to improve in Alfresco?
  • Can you recommend any Systems Integrator and freelancers who helped you to implement Alfresco in your way?
  • any other information you would like to share with us
